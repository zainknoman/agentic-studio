## Channel-Specific Notes

### LinkedIn
- Post between 08:00–10:00 or 17:00–18:30 in audience's timezone
- Hook in line 1 must stop the scroll — question, stat, or provocative statement
- End with a question to boost comments

### Twitter/X
- Threads perform best with 5–8 tweets; end with a clear CTA tweet
- Engage with replies within 2 hours of posting

### Blog / SEO
- Each article targets ONE primary keyword cluster from `keyword-clusters.md`
- Include FAQ section at the bottom targeting People Also Ask questions

### Email
- Subject line A/B test every send (pain-led vs benefit-led)
- Plain text outperforms HTML for B2B cold outreach; HTML for nurture

### Short-form video
- First 1.5 seconds determine watch-through — lead with the payoff, not the context
- Subtitles are mandatory (85% of mobile video is watched muted)
